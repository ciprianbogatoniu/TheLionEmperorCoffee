About Our Shop

First and foremost we are coffee lovers, coffee lovers that thought the world needs better coffee services. We are a small yet dedicated team, that thanks to our sponsors, have access to an incredibly wide range of coffee means to mix and match in order to offer you, our client, the best experience you can have.
